About Me

I am an assistant professor with the Department of Computer Science, Aalborg University, Denmark. I recieved the Ph.D. degree from Aalborg University, Denmark, in January 2022, under the supervision of Prof. Christian S. Jensen, Prof. Torben Bach Pedersen, and Prof. Lu Chen. From April 2018 to September 2018, I was a research assistant in Aalborg University, Denmark. I received the M.Sc. degree and the B.Sc. degree from Northeastern University, China, in 2017 and 2014, respectively.

Research Interests

My primary research areas are Database and Big Data Management and Analytics. Specifically, my current research interests are as follows:

  • Spatiotemporal Data Management and Analyticse
  • Big Graph Data Mining
  • Data Quality
  • Parallel and Distributed Processing
