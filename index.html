About me

I am a tenure-track assistant professor with Department of Computer Science, Aalborg University, Denmark. I am a member of Data Engineering, Science and Systems (DESS) group. I recieved the Ph.D. degree from Aalborg University, Denmark, in 2022, under the supervision of Prof. Christian S. Jensen, Prof. Lu Chen, and Prof. Torben Bach Pedersen. I won several awards including ICDE 2022 Best Paper (first author), NDBC 2022 One of the Best Papers, and ICCSIE 2022 Best Paper. I am the project co-investigator of Horizon European Framework Program (HORIZON) and Digital Research Centre Denmark Project (DIRECT).

Research Interests

My current research interests include:

  • Data Management and Analytics
  • Intelligent Transportation
  • Database Technology
  • Machine Learning
  • Complex Network
  • Digital Twin
